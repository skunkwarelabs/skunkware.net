key🅱️oard

A keyboard that replaces every B with 🅱️.

Source · Privacy GPLv3

key🅱️oard is an Android soft keyboard (an Input Method Editor) with one firm opinion: the letter B is 🅱️. Tap the B key, swipe out a word that contains a B, or hold backspace over one — either way you get the squared 🅱️, never a plain letter B.

What it does

Beyond the gimmick, it’s a real, full-featured keyboard:

  • Familiar layout. Three pages — letters, 123 numbers, and #+= symbols — switched with the 123 / #+= / ABC keys, plus a dedicated emoji page.
  • Material You theming. On Android 12+ the colors are pulled live from your system palette, with full light and dark support.
  • Glide typing. Swipe across the keys to type a word. There’s no Google dictionary to lean on, so key🅱️oard ships its own frequency-ranked wordlist and decodes each gesture with a SHARK²-style shape match. Any B in the result still comes out as 🅱️.
  • The little things. One-shot and locking shift, a rolling backspace that accelerates and then deletes whole words, per-key haptics, and a scrollable emoji picker.

Privacy

key🅱️oard works entirely on-device. It makes no network calls, has no API keys, and the only permission it requests is VIBRATE for key haptics. Nothing you type leaves your phone.
